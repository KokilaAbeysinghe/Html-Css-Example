They enrich soils, making the land around them good places to grow crops. Earthquakes signal the movement of Earth's tectonic plates,hich build mountains (including volcanoes). Natural disasters refer to environmental phenomenon that are destructive and occur naturally. Natural disasters include floods, volcanic activity, hurricanes, droughts, and earthquakes. While some of these disasters can be predicted and prepared for, they cannot be completely avoided and may result in damage. Some disasters can be so devastating that it wipes out human life in some areas.
